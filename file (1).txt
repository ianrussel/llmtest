 Elon Reeve Musk is a businessman and investor known for his key roles in space company SpaceX and automotive company Tesla, Inc. Other involvements include ownership of X Corp., formerly Twitter, and founding of The Boring Company .  As of June 2024, Forbes estimates his net worth to be US$210 billion . He is one of the wealthiest people in the world . He was born in Pretoria and briefly attended the University of Pretoria .  Zip2 was acquired by Compaq for $307 million in 1999 . That same year, Musk co-founded X.com, a direct bank . PayPal merged with Confinity in 2000 to form PayPal . In 2002, eBay acquired PayPal for $1.5 billion .  Musk founded SpaceX, a spaceflight services company, in 2002 . Using $100 million of the money he made from the sale of PayPal, he founded SpaceX .
